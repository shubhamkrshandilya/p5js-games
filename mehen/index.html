Mehen Rules (Snake Spiral)

1. Spiral Race: Players each control 3 normal marble pieces. Cast sticks (sums 1-5, with extra turns on 1, 4, 5) to move your pieces from the snake's tail (outermost space) along the spiral to the head (center space).

2. Lion Transformation: Once a piece reaches the snake's head (center), it transforms into a **Lion** (Hunter piece).

3. The Hunt: On subsequent turns, your Lion travels **backwards** from the head to the tail. The Lion captures (eats) any opponent normal pieces it lands on or passes over, removing them permanently from the game.

4. Win Condition: Win by either escaping your own pieces/Lions or capturing all opponent pieces with your Lions.
