Go Rules (9x9)

1. Placement: Take turns placing stones on line intersections (Black plays first). Stones cannot be moved once played.

2. Liberties: The adjacent empty points to a stone or connected group of the same color are its liberties. If a group has 0 liberties, it is captured and removed.

3. Suicide: You cannot place a stone that immediately has 0 liberties, unless that move captures opponent stones (which creates liberties).

4. Ko Rule: You cannot make a move that recreates the immediate previous board state (prevents infinite capture loops).
