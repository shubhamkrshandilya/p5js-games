Pente Grammai Rules

1. Goal: Position your pieces to capture opponent pieces or dominate the board. The first player to make 5 captures wins immediately.

2. Board: The board consists of 5 parallel lines with 5 intersections on each line. The center line is the **Sacred Line**.

3. Phase 1 (Placing): Players take turns placing their 5 pieces onto any empty intersection.

4. Phase 2 (Moving): Once all pieces are placed, players take turns moving one of their pieces to an adjacent empty intersection orthogonally.

5. Capturing: You capture an opponent's piece if you sandwich/custody it orthogonally between two of your pieces (similar to Hnefatafl captures).
