Patolli Rules (X-Shape Track)

1. Goal: Move all 6 of your pieces from the central node, travel around the 52-square X-shaped board track, and exit back at the center before the opponent does.

2. Bean Cast: Cast 5 black beans marked with white dots. Mouths/dots up score: 1 to 5 steps. If you roll 0 mouths up, you cannot move, and you must pay a token penalty.

3. Special Squares:
- *The Four Ends:* The terminal safe nodes on the arms of the cross.
- *Triangular Hazard Zones:* Landing on cells at the center intersections requires paying a coin penalty.

4. Captures: Landing on an opponent's piece captures it, sending it back to the start dock.
