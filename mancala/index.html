Mancala Rules

1. Sowing: Click on one of your active bottom pits to lift all seeds and sow them counter-clockwise one-by-one.

2. Store House: When sowing, a seed is placed in your right-side store. Skip the opponent's left store.

3. Extra Turn: If your last sown seed lands exactly in your right-side store, you immediately receive another turn.

4. Capture: If your last sown seed lands in an empty pit on your side, and the opposite opponent pit contains seeds, you capture all seeds from both pits.
