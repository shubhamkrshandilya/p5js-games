Backgammon Rules

1. Direction: You play as Cyan. Your checkers move left-to-right along the top row (from top-left point 23 to top-right point 12) and wrap down to move left-to-right along the bottom row to your home board (points 5-0). The Magenta AI moves right-to-left in the opposite direction.

2. Safe Points: A point with 2 or more checkers of the same color is secured. Opponent checkers cannot land on it.

3. Blot & Bar: A point with exactly 1 checker is a Blot. Landing on an opponent blot hits it, sending it to the central Bar. If you have checkers on the Bar, you must enter them into the opponent's home board (points 18-23) first.

4. Bearing Off: Once all 15 of your checkers reach your home board (points 5-0), you can clear them from the board. The first to bear off all 15 checkers wins.
