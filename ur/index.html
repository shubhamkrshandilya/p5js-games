Royal Game of Ur Rules

1. Goal: Race all 7 of your pieces along the track, entering the board, moving through the shared middle corridor, and exiting off your side track before the opponent.

2. Dice Cast: On your turn, roll 4 binary dice. The number of corners pointing up (0 to 4) is the number of spaces you must move a single piece.

3. Rosettes: Squares marked with a flower rosette are safe havens. Landing here awards an **extra dice roll** and makes your piece immune to capture.

4. Combat: Landing on an opponent's piece in the shared middle lane captures it, sending it back to the start. You cannot land on a rosette occupied by an opponent.

5. Bearing Off: To exit the board, you must roll the exact number of squares needed to move past the final square.
