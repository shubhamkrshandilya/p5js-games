Hounds and Jackals Rules

1. Goal: Move all 5 of your pegs along your designated side of the 58-hole board and exit from the final top space (Hole 29) before your opponent does.

2. Dice Cast: Roll standard dice. Movement matches rolled values.

3. Bridge shortcuts and traps:
- *Bridges (Shortcuts):* Landing on Hole 6 slides your peg ahead to Hole 20. Landing on Hole 8 slides you ahead to Hole 10.
- *Traps (Resets):* Landing on Hole 15 slides you back to Hole 9. Landing on Hole 25 slides you back to Hole 18.

4. Exit: You must roll an exact value to land on the final exit hole (Hole 29) and bear off.
