Nine Men's Morris Rules

1. Phase 1 (Placing): Players take turns placing their 9 stones on empty grid intersections. Try to form mills!

2. Phase 2 (Moving): Once all stones are placed, take turns sliding a stone along grid lines to an adjacent empty intersection.

3. Phase 3 (Flying): If a player is reduced to exactly 3 stones, they can fly their stones to any empty intersection on the board.

4. Mills & Capture: Aligning 3 of your stones in a straight line forms a Mill. This allows you to capture one of your opponent's stones (cannot capture stones in a mill unless only mills remain).
