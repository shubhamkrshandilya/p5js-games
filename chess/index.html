Chess Rules

1. Pieces: Pawns move 1 square forward (or 2 on start). Knights leap in L-shapes. Bishops slide diagonally. Rooks slide orthogonally. Queens move anywhere. Kings move 1 square.

2. Check: If your King is threatened, you must block, capture the attacker, or move your King to safety immediately.

3. Checkmate: If a King is in check and has no legal moves to escape, it is checkmated and the game ends.

4. Castling: A special king/rook defense move. Select the King, then move it 2 squares towards the Rook to execute.
