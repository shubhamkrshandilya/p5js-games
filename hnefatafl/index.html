Hnefatafl Rules (11x11)

1. Asymmetry: One player controls the Defenders (including the gold King in the center), and the other controls the Attackers surrounding them. You play as the Defenders.

2. Movement: Any piece can move any number of empty squares orthogonally (horizontal or vertical, like a Rook in Chess).

3. Restricted Squares: Only the King can stand on the central Throne or the four corner Escape squares.

4. Capture: An enemy piece is captured when sandwiched horizontally or vertically between two of your pieces (or one piece and an Escape corner/Throne).

5. Winning: Defenders win if the King reaches any of the four corner Escape squares. Attackers win if they surround the King on all 4 sides (or 3 sides if against the Throne or edge).
