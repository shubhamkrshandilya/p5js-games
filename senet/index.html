Senet Rules (3x10 S-Path)

1. Goal: Move all 5 of your pieces along the 30-square S-shaped track and bear them off from the last square before the opponent does.

2. Cast Sticks: Cast 4 flat sticks. Moving values: 1 flat side = 1 step, 2 = 2 steps, 3 = 3 steps, 4 = 4 steps, 0 flat sides = 5 steps. Rolls of **1, 4, and 5 grant an extra turn**.

3. Attacking/Swapping: Landing on an opponent's piece swaps places with it. However, if the opponent has two or more adjacent pieces of their color, they are **protected** and cannot be swapped.

4. Safe Houses:
- *Square 26 (House of Beauty):* Safe square. All pieces must stop here before passing.
- *Square 27 (House of Water):* Hazard square. Landing here resets your piece back to Square 15 (House of Rebirth).
- *Square 28, 29, 30:* Safe squares. Exact rolls of 3, 2, or 1 are required to bear off.
